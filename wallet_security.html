Wallet Security

To make withdrawals from your CoinRoster account, we need to have a wallet address on file for you. You can only have one address connected at a time, but you can change your address in certain circumstances.

Default security setting
By default, you can change your connected wallet address whenever you want. We tend to give users as much control as possible over their accounts.

Enabling security
After connecting a wallet you will have the option to enable wallet security, which will prevent you from changing your wallet address if you have Bitcoin in your account.

This is to prevent a would-be hacker from compromising your account and withdrawing your Bitcoins to a foreign wallet.

If you do enable security, you will need to withdraw all Bitcoins out of your account in order to change your wallet address.
